Rucksack MIA

Entry added on 16/10/2007 by Bart

Well we made it to Singapore safely...shame the same can't be said about by rucksack which is still missing in action. So if you see me wearing the same clothes in all the pictures then that's why :(
 
 On a more positive note, it's lovely and warm here and we've spent most of the day at Sentosa Island where we went on the skyride and luge. eV nearly toppled off after taking a bend too fast but he lives to see another day.
 
 On a less thrilling note, we've just been to M&S to pick me up some more pants :) Singapore is like a home from home really...same shops, everyone talking English, all the signs in English..the only strange thing seems to be that people leave a packet of tissues on cafe tables before they queue for their order to show that it's reserved, what's all that about?
