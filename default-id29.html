Its a small world...

Entry added on 17/11/2007 by eV

Traveling the world makes you realise actually how small it is, or more likely how people of the same cultures and social backgrounds holiday in the same spots. Still......
 
 Whilst in Hanmer Springs sitting in the hottest Sulphur pool; I mentioned to Ann-Marie that it smelt just like the famous Harrogate (spring) egg water. Two guys just getting in overheard me; and funnily enough one of them (Ben) was from Harrogate. Small world huh? Whilst we were chatting away about all things Yorkshire, his mate (Oliver) and Ann-Marie got talking and discovered that they are both starting at Accenture in January on the same day. Smaller world huh?
 
 So Ann-Marie has made her first work buddy (in a pool in a small New Zealand thermal spa resort)
