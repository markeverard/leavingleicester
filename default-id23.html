Dolphin Encounter

Entry added on 15/11/2007 by Bart

Woo-hoooooooooooooooo! eV and I have just been swimming with dolphins & it was awesome! They were so close and there was loads of them. At one point I had about 5 of them swimming underneath me and showing off!...they got so close and if you hold their attention they play with you and go round you in circles and as you try to copy and rotate with them they change directions, so basically you look like floundering fools but it was cool!
 
 I was making tooting noises which eV said scared him....he on the otherhand was singing barry manilow to them..lucky dolphins :)
 
 Pitty we cant find a cable to remove our snorkelling photos from the underwater camera as we would've got some awesome shots...instead you'll have to make do with the piccies we took from the boat :)
 
 Such good fun...would love to do it again!
