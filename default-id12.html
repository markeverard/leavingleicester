Who the F*** is Alice?

Entry added on 01/11/2007 by eV

(edit: I've just added a link to the photos)
 
 We've arrived in Alice Springs to be greeted by their first rainfall since March. Its a strange but nice place. I would have had some good pictures to show but I've forgotten to bring the memory card with me!
 
 For those of you wondering; I have made a decision about climbing Ayers Rock, thanks to a guy that shared our taxi to the airport in Cairns this morning. He used to work as a tour guide down at Uluru and gave some pretty compelling advice not to climb the rock. (I can't remember exactly what it was - but I can remember that it had me convinced!).
 
 As our lonely Planet guide puts it - you wouldn't climb all over the altar at Notre Dame - or walk through a mosque during prayer time (Actually I can imagine Dave Wilson doing that) so why would you climb over another religions sacred place.
 
 I feel a better man for making that decision. I might not be able to get a good view from the top of the rock, but the view from the moral high ground is superb!
