Rafting - white

Entry added on 29/11/2007 by eV

Rotorua AKA Roto-vegas, the home of more crazy extreme activities. This time Bart and I decided we would partake in Rafting of the white water kind and also zorbing - (which for those of you not in the know is pretending to be a hamster and climbing in a big ball and rolling yourself down a hill).
 
 I also unwittingly took part in the most extreme of all activities, one that produced a multitude of contrasting emotions. I call this activity losing the rental car keys.
 
 The day started with a gentle raft along the grade 5 Kaituna river and over the highest commercially rafted waterfall in the world (7 metres). It all seemed fairly straightforward until we looked at the photos! Have a look to see what I mean! Anyway we survived and headed back to the hostel in order to refuel with a warrior breakfast.
 
 We headed out to become zorb-anauts in the afternoon. We had to 'travel like commoners' and take the bus whilst the rental car stayed at the hostel. (note: by this time we'd located the car keys back at the rafting base but had to wait for them to be dropped off to us).
 
 Zorbing is as stupid as it looks.
