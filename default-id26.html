How to charm a dolphin

Entry added on 15/11/2007 by eV

If ever any of you are lucky enough to swim with dolphins then these tips may come in handy.
 
 Dolphins are intelligent creatures. Treat them with respect. To gain their attention you must woo them appropriately. Making silly noises will only serve to scare not only the dolphins but also the fellow humans who are swimming with you.
 
 Instead you must try some of the greatest love songs of our time but with one exception.
 
 Songs dolphins especially like:
 * Close to you - The Carpenters - This is particularly effective
 For a more upbeat song try:
 * Copacabana - Barry Manilow
 
 However under no circumstances sing Barry White. Although he wrote some of the most effective human love songs of all time they do not work on dolphins. The reason for this is that Barry White looked too much like a Walrus (a natural enemy of the Dolphin)
 
 I hope this helps
