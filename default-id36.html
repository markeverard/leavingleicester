You Can't Beat Wellington.......

Entry added on 29/11/2007 by eV

Wayne and Ned kindly offered to pick us up from Wellington airport at 730am on a Saturday morning and Wayne certainly looked like he'd enjoyed his Friday night.
 
 We got back to Casa del Waynedda at about 830am and promptly cracked open some breakfast beers, fired up the BBQ and were cooked a fry up, on the condition that my beard was dismantled. After the fuzz had gone we sat down to watch England - Croatia game that Wayne had recorded. This necessitated some more beer and so the tone of the day was set.
 
 In the afternoon we drove out to Eastbourne to grab a quick beer before coming back home for some more beer before going out to a comedy night at a local bar highlighting the best of local Wellington comedy talent. The night ended somehow.
 
 The next day was relaxed, with a trip to climb up to a lookout over Wellington, followed by a quick trip back down and a beer or two to rehydrate.
 
 Mmmm beer.
