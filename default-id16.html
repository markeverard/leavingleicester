I've seen sunshineeeeeee

Entry added on 08/11/2007 by eV

Yes, you read that correctly. The sun has got his hat on and is peeking out from behind the clouds. Its just a shame that this is the last day of our Sydney adventure.
 
 I've added some photos from the last three days, as Bart described in her last post. First up is the ferry ride and walk around Manly beach.
 
 Next up is pictures from the evening where we went bat hunting in the Botanical gardens. Bats are great, especially when playing cricket.
 
 Finally there are photos from our day trip to the Blue mountains, plenty of shots of kangeroos and my new favorite marsupial, a wombat (which I have renamed a pouched badger)
 
 You can clearly see that the Blue Mountains were more grey than blue, still the clouds did part to allow one or two nice shots. We had a quick stop at a winery on the way back where Bart managed to get drunk on 5 very small samples of wine.
